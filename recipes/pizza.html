Pizza Toast

We all love pizza, but sometimes we just donâ€™t have the time to make and bake a pizza dough or order and wait on pizza delivery. This pizza toast can be made in 20 minutes and we all love it!

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread
  • Pizza sauce or tomato sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Pepperoni and/or square-cut hams
  • Pineapple (optional)

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven and toast the slices of bread
  2. Spread each slice with the sauce and sprinkle the cheese
  3. Here, you can decide whatever kind of pizza you'd like to make! Add pepperonis for a pepperoni pizza, or ham and pineapple pieces for a hawaiian one.
  4. You can add a little bit of cheese afterwards for a grated top
  5. Put the slices of bread in a tray and inside the oven
  6. Remove until the cheese is melted or grated

Your pizza toasts are ready!
