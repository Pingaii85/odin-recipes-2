Baked Oatmeal

Baked oats are just like eating cake for breakfast. If you love eating oats in the mornings for a nutritious breakfast, but are looking to shake off things a little bit, this recipe is for you.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oat flour. You can also blend oats if you don-t have
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of baking flour
  • 1 tbsp of cocoa powder (Optional)
  • Chocolate chips (Optional)

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Mash half the banana. Slice the other half and save it for later.
  3. Add and mix the oats, the milk, the baking powder and the egg. If you want a chocolate flavor, also add the cocoa powder. (You can also use a blender)
  4. Grease a ramekin, you can use oil spray or melted butter. Pour the mix.
  5. Add the sliced banana and/or chocolate chips on top
  6. Put the ramekin in the oven until a toothpick comes clean

Now you can enjoy your cake for breakfast!
